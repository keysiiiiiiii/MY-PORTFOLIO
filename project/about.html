My story

My path into technology started with curiosity — a desire to understand how things work and how they could work better. That curiosity became a discipline: building systems that solve real problems for real teams.

As an HR & Payroll Assistant I saw firsthand how manual, repetitive processes slow teams down. I turned that frustration into action — configuring biometric systems, automating attendance and OJT tracking, and reducing hours of manual payroll work into reliable workflows.

That same instinct drove my biggest builds: Bio-ALMS, a biometric attendance and leave management system where I contributed 85% of the codebase, and BUBBL, a personal finance app I founded and shipped from zero.

I don't separate "technical" from "business" — every system I build starts with a real operational problem and ends with a measurable improvement. My goal as an aspiring IT Consultant is to keep doing exactly that, at greater scale.
